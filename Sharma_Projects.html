Scratch Project

August-September 2017

The goal of our scratch project was to make a game that would switch from backdrop to backdrop. The character was a ball and the at the end of every level there was an arrow -that would cause the backdrop to change. Along the levels there were little orange platforms for your character to rest on. Since there were an infinite amount of levels we wanted to see the amount of levels passed, but sadly we could not create a level counter. This project required skills such as teamwork. MIT App Inventor

October - November 2017

The objective of our MIT App Inventor project was to make a game in which you are a rocket and have to shoot the saucer down. You could control the rocket by moving it side to side while it shoots bullets. The saucer would move randomly move across the screen while you try to shoot bullets at it. You could play the game as long as you wished and the amount of points would be tracked. This project skills like teamwork and patience. Overall it was a fun and amusing project.
